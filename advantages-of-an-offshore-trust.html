Advantages of an Offshore Trust

Offshore trusts have significant financial and commercial benefits. They are legal entities, subject to fewer common law restrictions. They are important for tax-efficient investing and asset protection.

These are the main advantages of setting up an offshore trust:

  • Wealth and asset protection
  • Customisable according to the founder/family's needs
  • Tax-efficient investing
  • Property and capital gains tax planning tool
  • Commercially for employee pension and share schemes, financing and insurance
  • Legally recognised entity

These are examples of assets that Redwood clients hold in offshore trusts:

  • Shares in quoted and unquoted companies
  • Investment portfolios
  • Cash deposits
  • Property
  • Life assurance policies issued on the life of the settlor
  • Art
  • Commodities
  • Intellectual property
